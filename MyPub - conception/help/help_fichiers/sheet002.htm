Frequently Asked Questions
User
#01
To create an account,  fill in the requested information on the main web page.
#02
To update your profil, click on "profile" tab in the MyPub web page.
#03
To delete your profil, click on the red button on tab in the MyPub web page.
#04
To add a friend, find his profil on the friend web page via the search tab.
#05
To add an avatar, go to your profil page and click on "add avatar".
#06
To delete a friendly relationship, select the friend in his friend list (on profil page),
go to the profil page of your friend, and click on remove tab.
